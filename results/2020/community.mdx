# 6.Community

Open-source culture and sharing knowledge are gaining a lot of popularity in Morocco lately(<a target='_blank' href='https://www.htxt.co.za/2019/11/11/african-countries-are-the-fastest-growing-github-contributors/#:~:text=Morocco'>African countries are the fastest growing GitHub contributors</a>) . More than 80% of respondents have at least contributed to an open-source project. Also, Around 75% are sharing their findings and learning through blogging.

### Are you part of any local developer's community?

We don't know if people misunderstood this question or that is actually what the reality is! But more than 70% claim that they're not part of any developers community. Even more troubling, because a majority of respondents claimed they are part of the Dev Circle Morocco community ¯\_(ツ)\_/¯.

### Did you ever participate in an open-source project?

Moroccan developers contribute more and more to open source, with more than 50% said they are maintaining their own projects.

### Did you have the chance to write blog posts?

While 25% of respondents are not interested in blogging, three-quarters of respondents are already bloggers or trying to make their first steps.

### How many tech events did you attend in 2019/2020?

More than half of the respondents attended at least one tech event in the past two years!

<Chart id="community-q-3" title={false} />

### How many talks did you participate in as a speaker in 2019/2020?

15% of respondents have already spoken at some IT event!

### Online vs in-person events?

The Moroccan community is eager to learn and discover new things. Almost 70% said that the event format doesn't make a difference ("Li ja" for the non-dialect speaking meaning "whatever").

<Chart id="community-q-5" title={false} />

### Are you part of one of the Moroccan Facebook Developer circle groups?

This survey is an initiative from the Developer Circle Morocco community. More than 55% of respondents are already part of it!

<Chart id="community-q-6" title={false} />

### How do you evaluate the Moroccan Tech Community?

More than 93% of all respondents think that the Moroccan community is quite good!
